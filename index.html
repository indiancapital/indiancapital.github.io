Who We Are

Founded in 1995, Indian Capital and Investment (Cbe) Ltd. is a Non-Banking Financial Company (NBFC) specializing in proprietary investments. Since inception, we have exclusively invested our own capital. Our disciplined approach and strategic foresight have enabled us to navigate market complexities while focusing on long-term value creation.


As financial markets evolve, we are actively exploring advanced technology, AI-driven models, and algorithmic investment strategies to enhance decision-making and optimize capital deployment. By combining strategic investment expertise with emerging financial technologies, we aim to strengthen our position in the industry and adapt to the future of investing.
